How to Make Turkey Burgers

The good news is that making turkey burgers from scratch is quite easy. All you need is ground turkey, onion, bread crumbs, eggs, and some seasonings and you're good to go. Simply combine your ground turkey, diced onion, bread crumbs, egg whites, garlic, parsley, salt, and ground pepper in a bowl. We recommend sticking with ground turkey that's about 93% lean or less — any leaner than that and your burger might fall apart. Form the ground turkey mixture into patties and cook the burgers using your preferred method.

Ingredients:

  • 3 pounds ground turkey
  • ¼ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup finely diced onion
  • 2 egg whites, lightly beaten
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix ground turkey, seasoned bread crumbs, onion, egg whites, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  3. Form into 12 patties.
  4. Cook the patties in a medium skillet over medium heat, turning once, to an internal temperature of 180 degrees F (85 degrees C).
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
